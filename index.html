Rock, Paper, Scissors!

Game Rules

You will compete against the computer.

Choose between Rock, Paper, and Scissors.

The first to score three points wins.

Scoring points in the game works as follows:

  • Rock defeats Scissors
  • Scissors defeats Paper
  • Paper defeats Rock

If both the player and the computer select the same option (e.g., Paper and Paper), no points are awarded.
